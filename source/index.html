Every woman has a night-time alter ego inside of her.
It steals the blanket and occupies the bed’s territory; sleep-walks to the fridge and twitches before falling back to sleep. Sometimes it sings (or maybe it snores?) And the 8th of March is the best day to get to know that alter ego!

Solution

The Askona brand and digital agency Nectarin offered their users
to find out once and for all who awakens in a woman whilst she is asleep!
Depending on the result based on an illustration and real-life situations, the user will be offered
a perfect gift from the brand that they will be able to present to their lady.

We created a landing page with a simple test test.askona.ru
The user answers a number of questions (including funny ones) – these questions are about what women prefer in their sleep: positions, smells and so on. At the end of the test you get a result with a special “type” of woman with a fitting illustration and a suitable present for her for the 8th of March out of Askona’s product range. The test can be taken by men and they will find out about their women’s type and the perfect present for her for the 8th of March. However, women can also take the test get the result and this way – drop a “delicate” hint to their soulmate.
